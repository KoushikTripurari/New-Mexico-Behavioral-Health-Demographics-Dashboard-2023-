Complex Case Discharge Delay — Pilot Instructions

Quick-start steps, reporting cadence, flip-card definitions, and contacts — in a turquoise, full-width layout.

1) Collect data internally

Include cases that meet all criteria below; you may optionally include shorter delays.
  • Avoidable delay: > 96 hours (ED) and/or > 336 hours (inpatient).
  • Period: Present between April 1 – June 30.
  • Exclusion: Not in a swing bed.

2) Get portal access

  1. Email complexcase@hanys.org to request access.
  2. Activate your account via the Okta link (check spam if needed).
  3. If not activated within 7 days, request a new activation email.

3) Report cases (monthly)

Report at least once a month (e.g., by May 9, June 8, July 8).

How to add a case

  1. Sign in to the pilot application.
  2. Choose Case Management.
  3. Select your Hospital Name.
  4. Click Add New Case and complete required fields.
  5. Click Save.

You’ll be logged out after 15 minutes of inactivity; incomplete cases appear highlighted on the homepage.
